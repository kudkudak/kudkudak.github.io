I am a 3rd year PhD student @ Jagiellonian University supervised by Prof. Jacek Tabor and co-advised by Prof. Amos Storkey (University of Edinburgh). Adviser to startups (in stealth mode :) ). I spent two summers as a visiting student with Prof. Yoshua Bengio. Prior to that I completed industrial internships with Palantir and Microsoft.

My email is staszek.jastrzebski (on gmail).

My main research goal is to understand how deep network generalize. My research interests include:

  • Optimization in Deep Learning
  • Representation Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Aided Drug Design

Co-supervised MSc/BSc students:

  • [MSc] Andrii Krutsylo - Physics aware representation for drug discovery
  • [MSc] Tomasz Wesołowski - Relevance of enriching word embeddings in modern deep natural language processing
  • [BSc] Michał Soboszek - Evaluating word embeddings
  • [Defended, MSc] Jakub Chłędowski - Representation learning for textual entailment
  • [Defended, MSc] Mikołaj Sacha - Meta learning and sharpness of the minima
